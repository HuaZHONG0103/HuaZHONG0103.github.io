# Hua Zhong, Ph.D.

**Postdoctoral Research Fellow**  
**LSU Health Sciences Center**

**Statistical Genetics | Multi-omics | AI | Cancer Genomics**

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at LSU Health Sciences Center. My research focuses on statistical genetics, multi-omics integration, artificial intelligence, and cancer genomics. I develop and apply computational approaches to identify disease-associated genes, regulatory mechanisms, molecular biomarkers, and population-specific biological pathways.

## Research Highlights

- **Statistical genetics and fine-mapping:** developing and applying genetic models to identify disease-associated genes, regulatory variants, and causal molecular mechanisms.
- **Multi-omics integration:** integrating genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, single-cell, and spatial transcriptomics to study human disease biology.
- **AI-assisted genomics:** applying machine learning and large language model-assisted approaches to prioritize functional variants, genes, and molecular targets.
- **Cancer genomics:** investigating molecular mechanisms, biomarkers, and population-specific disease risk across prostate cancer, breast cancer, glioma, and other cancers.
